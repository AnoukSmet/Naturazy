Welcome to Naturazy

About Naturazy

Waking up in the morning in a tree house is an amazing experience. Being closer to nature including the trees, birds, and other wildlife can revitalize the spirit. But, being at a tree house bed and breakfast can make the adventure much more exciting. Although, we give each of our tree house guests their fair share of privacy each night they stay with us, we also want to make sure that they get the royal treatment as well. Our breakfasts are served with love everyday and give us the joy of meeting each of you face to face and sharing our lives with each other. Come and discover this unique experience!

About us

We’ve been in the hospitality business for quite a while and we know the importance of meeting face to face and serving on a personal level. We had longed dreamed of opening a bed and breakfast that would fuse Mark's passion for treehouses, Karin’s knack for hosting and cooking, and our shared love of nature. When we came across four acres for sale near our home in the the Ardennes, we knew the time was ripe to bring their dream to life. In 2006 we officialy opened Naturazy and welcomed our first guests. Thank you for visiting Naturazy! We strive to make you feel at home in the trees and to celebrate life, love, and nature. Mark & Karin

Discover our Tree Houses

Our Tree Houses are spacious enough to move about easily, is surrounded by windows, and has a queen size bed. The main level has a beautiful built-in lounging area surrounded by windows, a dining room area with microwave, Keurig and mini fridge, and a bathroom with shower, sink and toilet. The tree house has a back deck complete with Adirondack chairs and a walkway that wraps around three sides of the treehouse. It’s the perfect place to rest, relax, and reconnect with nature and loved ones.
